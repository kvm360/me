Mission & Vision

Diagnostic precision,
delivered across India

Stethix Healthcare was founded with one conviction: that every veterinary practice in India from a single-vet clinic in Kerala's backwaters to a large corporate animal hospital in Mumbai or Delhi deserves access to world-class diagnostic instruments at transparent pricing, with real after-sales support.

We supply veterinary diagnostic equipment from 10+ premium global brands including HORIBA, Mindray, Abbott iSTAT, Pushkang and Bionote. Our team is based in Kerala, Telangana and Karnataka, and we regularly supply customers across 18+ states small single-vet clinics, multi-speciality animal hospitals, veterinary colleges and large corporate hospital chains.

Genuine Products, Real Support — All equipment supplied by Stethix Healthcare carries full manufacturer warranty with genuine reagents and genuine spare parts. We do not deal in grey-market or refurbished equipment.
Same-Day Response — Quote requests via WhatsApp are typically responded to within the same working day, with on-site demos arranged wherever possible across India.

Our core values

Every interaction, every instrument delivered and every after-sales visit is shaped by these principles.

Trust & Authenticity

Every instrument is genuine, authorised and backed by the manufacturer. We never compromise on the integrity of our supply chain.

Clinical Precision

Common Questions

About Stethix Healthcare

Where is Stethix Healthcare headquartered?

Stethix Healthcare LLP is headquartered in Ernakulam, Kerala, India (PIN 682036). The company has additional operational offices in Telangana and Karnataka to serve customers across India more effectively.

How many states does Stethix Healthcare supply to?

Stethix Healthcare regularly supplies veterinary diagnostic equipment to customers across 18+ states in India including Kerala, Telangana, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra, Delhi, Gujarat, Rajasthan, West Bengal and many more. We serve small clinics and large corporate animal hospitals alike.

What brands of veterinary equipment does Stethix Healthcare supply?

Stethix Healthcare supplies equipment from 10+ premium global brands including HORIBA, Mindray, Abbott iSTAT, Pushkang and Bionote. Product categories include hematology analysers, biochemistry systems, point-of-care / blood gas devices, progesterone analysers, lab consumables and veterinary laser therapy equipment.

When was Stethix Healthcare founded?

Stethix Healthcare LLP was founded in 2024 and rapidly built its supply network across India. Despite being a young company, our team brings years of biomedical engineering, hospital technology and veterinary distribution experience.

Do you serve both small clinics and large corporate hospitals?

Yes. Stethix Healthcare serves the full range of veterinary practices from single-vet clinics and small animal hospitals to large corporate veterinary hospital chains and government veterinary institutions across India. Our equipment and support are tailored to each practice's size and requirements.

What languages does your team speak?

Our team is fluent in English, Malayalam, Telugu, Kannada, Tamil and Hindi so we can communicate with customers across India without any language barrier. WhatsApp communication is available in all these languages.
