What We Publish

The kind of content
this blog is built for

We publish useful, honest, field-tested knowledge not marketing content. If it helps a vet somewhere in India make a better decision, it belongs here.

Clinical Case Reports

Documented cases with diagnostic workup, test results, differentials and treatment outcomes especially unusual presentations or underreported conditions in India.

Research & Study Findings

Original research from veterinary colleges, institutions or field studies prevalence data, treatment comparisons, diagnostic accuracy findings, or population-level observations.

Treatment Protocols

Practical, step-by-step treatment protocols refined through real clinical experience particularly those adapted for the Indian veterinary context or resource-limited settings.

Equipment Guides & Reviews

Honest evaluations of diagnostic equipment analyser performance in real-world Indian clinic conditions, reagent reliability, maintenance experience and value assessments.

Lab QC & Best Practices

Quality control protocols, sample handling tips, calibration schedules, rejection reduction strategies and in-house lab best practices from experienced veterinary lab professionals.

Field Knowledge from Indian Vets

Observations, insights or lessons from practice in India regional disease patterns, breed-specific considerations, wildlife encounters or rural clinic challenges worth documenting.
