Common Questions

Contact & Enquiry FAQ

What is the fastest way to reach Stethix Healthcare?

WhatsApp at +91 91084 47768 is the fastest way. Our team monitors WhatsApp throughout working hours and typically responds within the same working day. You can also use the enquiry form on this page — it sends your message directly to WhatsApp.

How does the enquiry form work?

When you click "Send Enquiry via WhatsApp", your form details are compiled into a structured message and WhatsApp opens with it pre-filled. You just tap Send — no email, no login, no waiting for a confirmation. It reaches us instantly.

Can I request a demo of equipment before buying?

Yes. We arrange on-site or clinic-based demos for HORIBA, Mindray, Abbott iSTAT and other analysers across India. Select "Equipment Demo / Trial" in the form or mention it in your WhatsApp message and we'll coordinate a visit.

Do you supply outside Kerala, Telangana and Karnataka?

Yes — we regularly supply veterinary diagnostic equipment to 18+ states across India including Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Delhi, Gujarat, Rajasthan, West Bengal, Odisha, Assam and more. Select your state in the form and we'll coordinate accordingly.

In which languages can I contact your team?

Our team communicates in English, Malayalam, Telugu, Kannada, Tamil and Hindi. WhatsApp us in whichever language you're most comfortable with and we'll respond in kind.

From a single CBC machine to a fully equipped lab we'll find the right solution for your practice and budget. Let's talk.
