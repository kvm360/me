What is the HORIBA ABX Micros ESV 60 used for in veterinary medicine?

The HORIBA ABX Micros ESV 60 is a veterinary hematology analyser for CBC testing in animals — 21 parameters including a 3-part white cell differential, processing 60 samples per hour. Used in veterinary hospitals, polyclinics and independent clinics for routine diagnostics and disease monitoring.
