What Makes It Different

Precise 5-Part Differential, Built for Every Species

Tri-Angle Laser Scatter Technology

Laser scatter combined with chemical dye and flow cytometry gives better 5-part WBC differentiation, even on samples with high Eosinophil counts.

Independent Basophil Channel

A dedicated optical channel and method for Basophil measurement, separate from the main WBC differential pathway, for more reliable counts.

Minimal Sample Volume

Aspirates only 15μL of whole blood in whole-blood mode, or 20μL of capillary blood in prediluted mode — ideal for small or exotic animals.

High Throughput

Processes up to 60 samples per hour, so busy veterinary diagnostic labs can keep pace with sample volume without compromising turnaround time.

Broad Species Library

13 built-in animal species plus 20 self-programmable animal profiles — one analyzer for mixed-species veterinary and exotic-pet practices.

Large Touchscreen & Storage

A 10.4-inch large TFT touchscreen for simple operation, with storage for up to 40,000 results including numeric and graphic information.

Bi-Directional LIS Connectivity

Supports bi-directional Laboratory Information System integration, enabling seamless data exchange with other clinic or lab IT systems.

Cyanide-Free Reagent System

Hemoglobin measurement uses a cyanide-free colorimetric method, paired with electrical impedance counting for RBC and PLT — safer day-to-day handling.
