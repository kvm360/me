What Makes It Different

Built for Speed, Precision & Small Sample Volumes

Electronic Impedance Method

Precise cellular identification through electronic impedance variation, the reference method for enumerating RBCs, WBCs and platelets.

Photometric Haemoglobin

Haemoglobin measurement using photometry alongside impedance counting for a complete, accurate cell count profile.

External Barcode Reader

Instant identification of patient samples, QC and reagents — reduces manual entry errors in busy clinics.

Colour Touchscreen

User-friendly LCD colour touch screen with virtual keyboard makes daily operation simple for clinic staff.

USB Data Transfer

Archive results to PC and upload QC target values via USB key — no complex network setup required.

Trusted Japanese Technology

Engineered by HORIBA, Japan — a globally trusted name in precision measurement and diagnostic instrumentation for over 70 years.
