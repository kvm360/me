Editorial Standards

Submission guidelines

We keep the bar high so the platform stays trustworthy for everyone reading it — including the vets relying on it for clinical decisions.

Original work only. No content published elsewhere or AI-generated without disclosed human review.
800–2,000 words. Long enough to say something real, short enough to respect the reader's time.
Cite your sources. Clinical claims and statistics need a reference — journal, study, or institutional data.
Plain, precise language. Written for both fellow professionals and informed pet parents — explain jargon where it appears.
Include your credentials. Degree, institution/clinic, and registration number (if applicable) for your author bio.
Visuals welcome. Original photos, diagrams, or charts strengthen a submission — just confirm you own the rights.
How It Works

From draft to published

Write your draft

Use our guidelines above. A clear title and a one-line summary of your key takeaway help us route it faster.

Email it to us

Send your piece as a Word doc or Google Doc link to info@stethix.com with the subject line "Write For Us Submission" — include your credentials and a short bio.

Editorial review

Our team reviews for accuracy, originality, and clarity. We may suggest edits or ask clarifying questions — typically within 7–10 days.

Get published

Your article goes live on vet.stethix.com with full byline credit, your bio, and optional links to your clinic, institution, or social profiles.
