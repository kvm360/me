The Process

What happens after
you send your resume

Simple, transparent, and respectful of your time. Here's exactly what to expect when you email us.

1

Email your resume

Send your CV as a PDF or Word file to info@stethix.com with the subject line "Resume — [Your Role Interest]". A short cover note about yourself is always appreciated.

2

We save your profile

Our team reviews every resume received and saves it in our internal talent file against the relevant role category sales, technical, logistics or operations.

3

We reach out when a role opens

When a position that matches your background becomes available, we reach out directly by email or phone. Roles are typically field-based in Kerala, Telangana or Karnataka, with pan-India opportunities as we grow.

4

Quick, personal conversation first

No lengthy application forms. Our first step is always a direct phone or WhatsApp call to understand what you're looking for and share what the role involves.

Common Questions

Careers FAQ

Are there any current job openings at Stethix Healthcare?

There are no current openings at Stethix Healthcare LLP. However, we actively keep a talent file of strong candidates. Send your resume to info@stethix.com and we will reach out as soon as a suitable role opens up.

How do I apply for a job at Stethix Healthcare?

Simply email your resume (PDF or Word) to info@stethix.com with the subject line "Resume — [Your Role Interest]". Include a short note about yourself and the type of role you're looking for. No forms, no portals — just a direct email.

What kind of roles does Stethix Healthcare hire for?

We typically hire for veterinary equipment sales, biomedical and service engineering, technical specialist roles, business development, logistics and digital marketing. Most roles are based in Kerala, Telangana or Karnataka, with field and remote opportunities across India.

What background is preferred for sales roles at Stethix Healthcare?

For sales roles, we prefer candidates with a background in biomedical engineering, veterinary science, life sciences, or prior experience in medical/veterinary equipment sales. A passion for animal healthcare and strong communication skills in regional languages are a plus.

Will I be notified when a job matching my profile opens?

Yes — that's exactly the point of sending your resume in advance. Once your profile is on file, our team will contact you directly by email or phone when a suitable role becomes available. You won't need to keep checking any job board.

Which locations does Stethix Healthcare hire in?

Our primary hiring locations are Kerala (headquartered in Ernakulam), Telangana (Hyderabad region) and Karnataka (Bengaluru and Mysuru). As we expand, roles open across Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu and other states where we supply veterinary equipment. Remote roles in digital and operations are also available.

Interested in what we do? Let's talk.

Even if there's no role today, reach out. The veterinary diagnostic equipment market in India is growing fast and we're growing with it.
